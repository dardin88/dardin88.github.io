Dario Di Nucci

I obtained my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Salerno, Italy, in 2014.
Currently I am research fellow in the Software Languages Lab at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel within the "INTelligent Modernisation Assistance for Legacy Software" (INTiMALS) project with Prof. Coen De Roover.
Moreover, I am working toward the Ph.D. degree at the University of Salerno, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Andrea De Lucia.
My research interests include software maintenance and evolution, software testing, search based software engineering, green mining, mining software repositories, and empirical software engineering.
